# Gujarat Vridha Pension Yojana (Senior Citizen Pension Scheme)

## Overview

The Government of Gujarat provides financial assistance to senior citizens through the **Vridha Pension Yojana**, also known as the **Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS)** or **Vayvandana Scheme**. This scheme aims to support elderly citizens who have no regular income source or financial backup.

---

## Monthly Pension Amount

| Age Group | Monthly Amount |
|-----------|----------------|
| 60-79 years | ₹1,000 |
| 80+ years | ₹1,250 |

The pension is transferred directly to the beneficiary's bank account through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

---

## Eligibility Criteria

- **Age**: 60 years and above
- **Income Limit**:
  - Urban residents: Less than ₹1,50,000 per year
  - Rural residents: Less than ₹1,20,000 per year
- **BPL Status**: Must be Below Poverty Line (BPL) with BPL score ≤ 20
- **Residency**: Must be a resident of Gujarat
- **Financial Condition**: Should have no regular income source or unable to earn

---

## Required Documents

1. Aadhaar Card
2. Age Certificate / Birth Certificate
3. Bank Passbook
4. Income Certificate
5. Ration Card
6. Election Card (Voter ID)
7. Passport Size Photo
8. BPL Card (if applicable)

---

## How to Apply

### Option 1: Online Application

1. **Visit Digital Gujarat Portal**
   - Go to: https://digitalgujarat.gov.in

2. **Register/Login**
   - If new, click "Citizen Registration" and create an account
   - If existing, login with username and password

3. **Find the Scheme**
   - Search for "Vridha Pension" or "Old Age Pension"
   - Or go to: Social Security → Pension Schemes → Apply

4. **Fill Application Form**
   - Enter personal details (name, age, address)
   - Enter bank account details (for DBT)
   - Enter income details

5. **Upload Documents**
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Age Certificate
   - Bank Passbook
   - Income Certificate
   - Ration Card
   - Passport size photo
   - BPL Card (if applicable)

6. **Submit Application**
   - Review all details
   - Click Submit
   - Note down the Application ID for tracking

### Option 2: Offline Application

1. **Visit Local Office**
   - Taluka/Tehsil Office
   - Gram Panchayat (for rural)
   - Municipal Office (for urban)

2. **Get Application Form**
   - Ask for "Vridha Pension Yojana" form

3. **Fill the Form**
   - Fill in all required details
   - Attach self-attested copies of documents

4. **Submit**
   - Submit to the concerned officer
   - Get receipt/acknowledgment with application number

### Option 3: Via Helpline
- Call **14567** and request assistance with application

---

## Track Application Status

1. Go to Digital Gujarat Portal
2. Click "Track Application"
3. Enter your Application ID
4. Check status (Approved/Rejected/Pending)

---

## Key Features

- **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)**: Payments directly credited to bank account
- **Service Delivery Time**: Approximately 15 days
- **No Occupation Required**: Available for all senior citizens meeting criteria
- **Gender**: Both Male and Female eligible

---

## Other Senior Citizen Benefits in Gujarat

1. **Bus Fare Discount**: Concession on Gujarat State Transport buses
2. **Train Ticket Concessions**: Senior citizen concessions on Indian Railways
3. **Free Medical Assistance**: Access to government medical facilities
4. **Priority Services**: Priority in government offices and services
5. **Senior Citizen Certificate**: Can be applied via Digital Gujarat or at local offices
